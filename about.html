About Stackly

25 years of pioneering medical device innovation — putting patients first, always.

Who We Are

Redefining Healthcare Through Precision Technology

Founded in 2001 in Boston, Massachusetts, Stackly began with a singular purpose: to build medical devices that speak the language of clinicians. From our first cardiac monitor to today's AI-integrated surgical robots, every product we create goes through 3,000+ hours of testing before it ever enters an operating room.

Today, Stackly operates in 60+ countries with over 2,800 employees — all united by an unshakeable belief that better tools lead to better care.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

2001

Founded in Boston

Three biomedical engineers launch Stackly with a seed-funded cardiac monitoring prototype.

2006

FDA Approval & First 50 Hospitals

Our CardioScan line receives FDA 510(k) clearance and enters 50 US hospital systems.

2011

Global Expansion

Opened regional offices in London, Dubai, and Singapore. Crossed 200 hospital partners.

2018

AI Integration Launch

Unveiled Stackly AI — real-time anomaly detection integrated into all new devices.
